about us

MyDigiLaw is a society of the world’s finest independent, boutique law firms. With nearly 50 member firms in Chennai, India. MyDigiLaw provides clients easy access to the right lawyer, with the right skills, in the right location, and at the right cost.

We seek out, accept and retain only the best firms for membership. Each firm is screened to ensure its commitment to excellence as embodied in the Six Pillars: Integrity, Excellent Work Product, Reasonable Fees, Continuing Legal Education, Civility and Community Service.
